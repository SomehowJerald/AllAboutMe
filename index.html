Find the Epic Faces Guide

Normal Epic Face

"sj_epicface:"

Rarity: "Epic"

Hidden In Spawn. Right Infront of you.

Burning Epic Face

"SOMEONE PUT THE FIRE OUT"

Rarity: Easy

Hidden In Lava. It is infront of the volcano, climb your way up to him.

Demon Epic Face

"Muhahahahhhhhhhh................"

Rarity: Hard

Hidden In Lava. When in lava, head over to the left side of the lava path, the epic face is hidden against the wall inside the edge of the lava path, try adjusting your camera angle to see where it is, once you find it...collect it!

Evil Epic Face

"bros the evil smiling imp"

Rarity: Challenging

Hidden in Lava, In Lava there is a rock that looks like the letter "H", a PURPLE button is hidden in the corner of the top right angle of the rock. Once you press it, next to the volcano it seems to be a rock opened up, inside the rock is Evil Epic Face.

Stone Epic Face

"he isnt hidden at all if hes on top of an obby is he"

Rarity: Medium

Hidden in Lava, Somewhat next to the volcano is a structure, the structure appears to be a obby, completing the obby will reward you Stone Epic Face. If you are good at obbies then this should be no problem, i also recommend turning your brightness up.

Teal Epic Face

"now this is a peak color"

Rarity: Medium

Hidden in Tundra. In the middle of the Tundra map there is a big rock structure with a campfire on the top, you need to climb your way to the top, like a mini obby
