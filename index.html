Find the Epic Faces Guide

Normal Epic Face

"sj_epicface:"

Rarity: Epic

Hidden In Spawn. Right Infront of you.

Burning Epic Face

"SOMEONE PUT THE FIRE OUT"

Rarity: Easy

Hidden In Lava. It is infront of the volcano, climb your way up to him.

Demon Epic Face

"Muhahahahhhhhhhh................"

Rarity: Hard

Hidden In Lava. When in lava, head over to the left side of the lava path, the epic face is hidden against the wall inside the edge of the lava path, try adjusting your camera angle to see where it is, once you find it...collect it!

Evil Epic Face

"bros the evil smiling imp"

Rarity: Challenging

Hidden in Lava, In Lava there is a rock that looks like the letter "H", a PURPLE button is hidden in the corner of the top right angle of the rock. Once you press it, next to the volcano it seems to be a rock opened up, inside the rock is Evil Epic Face.

Stone Epic Face

"he isnt hidden at all if hes on top of an obby is he"

Rarity: Medium

Hidden in Lava, Somewhat next to the volcano is a structure, the structure appears to be a obby, completing the obby will reward you Stone Epic Face. If you are good at obbies then this should be no problem, i also recommend turning your brightness up.

Teal Epic Face

"now this is a peak color"

Rarity: Medium

Hidden in Tundra. In the middle of the Tundra map there is a big rock structure with a campfire on the top, you need to climb your way to the top but dont go all the way up, it is like a mini obby. Once you are up there the Epic Face is hidden infront of a rock near a tree, it might take a while to find but dont worry.

Snow Epic Face

"why does it look like a collapsed snowman :C"

Rarity: Easy

Hidden In Tundra, Head over to your right and climb up the small structure, it should be at the top.

Frozen Epic Face

"why was dynamite used and not something like a flamethower bruh bad game i cant believe this"

Rarity: Hard

Start in Desert Base, Head over to the "SALOON". Once you are in there, check behind the counter and there should be dynamite, Click on it and you should hear a explosion noise, Then head over to "Tundra". Once you are in Tundra, follow the path and head over to the cave. The Epic Face should be in there

Blue Epic Face

"I'm blue da ba dee da ba di da ba dee da ba di da ba dee da ba di da ba dee da ba di da ba dee da ba di da ba dee da ba di da ba dee da ba di"

Hidden in Spawn, Climb up the blue structure to the right, Once at the top there should be a hidden green button around there, once you click it a path will open up but once again you have to find another green button, the green button is located underneath a edge of the blue structure, once you click it another path should open up and you can claim the Blue Epic Face

Red Epic Face

"every hell level cmon man why cant it be orange"

Rarity: Easy

Hidden In Lava, Once in Lava head over to your right, the Epic Face should be inside the corner of the lava path.

Skull Epic Face

"im not doing it"

Rarity: Challenging

Start at Desert Base, In the left side there is a codepad that opens up a secret area, type in the codepad "19582" after that the secret area should open up. Once you are in the area, keep takings rights until you are in a long hallway, once in the hallway keep going until you reach a Deadend, now the "Deadend" is actually a obby now once you finish the obby Skull Epic Face should be at the end of the obby.
